Why OESA Exists

OESA proposes a transition from disposable satellites to maintainable orbital infrastructure. It treats Earth orbit as a shared environment requiring ongoing service capability rather than occasional intervention.

Architecture Overview

The Orbital Environmental and Sustainability Architecture is designed as a permanent ecosystem, not a single mission.
